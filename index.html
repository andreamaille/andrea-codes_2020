About Me.

Nice to meet you!

I’m Andrea and I’m a front-end web developer living in Toronto, ON. My journey into web development all started with my Etsy Store when I wanted to build my own website. One google search ended up with me finding my dream career.

I graduated from Juno College’s Immersive Web Development Program in 2019 and currently work at Commerce Dynamics developing ecommerce and loyalty websites for major league sports and airline rewards programs.

When I’m not coding, you can catch me listening to k-pop or late 90’s pop punk, playing cello, catching up on my favourite podcasts or bullet journalling.
