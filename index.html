About Me

Hi, I'm Andrea!

I’m a front-end web developer living in Toronto, ON. My journey into web development all started with my Etsy Store when I wanted to build my own website. One google search ended up with me finding my dream career. I've always been a creative person and when I discovered coding, I realized I had discovered the ultimate creative experience. I love seeing art, music, sound, movement, design and creative thinking all come together to create new spaces online.

I currently work with the lovely folks at Commerce Dynamics to create great ecommerce experiences for fortune

When I’m not coding, you can catch me listening to k-pop or late 90’s pop punk, playing cello, catching up on my favourite podcasts or bullet journalling.
